About the Firm

Roman Ratmansky, Adv. focuses on contract and claim management in large-scale infrastructure and building projects. We combine deep understanding of construction processes with precise legal analysis, helping clients reduce risk, prevent disputes, and protect their commercial interests throughout the project lifecycle.

Core Services

  • Drafting and negotiating EPC, design-build, and construction contracts.
  • Ongoing claim management: delay, disruption, scope changes, and cost escalation.
  • Risk allocation and contract strategy for public and private tenders.
  • Representation in negotiations, mediation, and arbitration in construction disputes.
