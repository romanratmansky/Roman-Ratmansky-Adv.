About the Firm

Roman Ratmansky, Adv. provides specialized legal services in contract and claim management for major infrastructure and construction projects. With deep familiarity in engineering processes, project execution, and commercial risk, the firm delivers precise legal strategies that prevent disputes, streamline project delivery, and protect client interests throughout the entire project lifecycle.

Core Services

  • Drafting, reviewing, and negotiating EPC, design‑build, and construction contracts.
  • Ongoing claim management: delays, disruptions, scope changes, and cost escalation.
  • Risk allocation strategies for public and private tenders.
  • Representation in negotiations, mediation, and arbitration.
  • Contractual support for project managers, contractors, and developers.
  • Legal oversight during project execution and interface coordination.
